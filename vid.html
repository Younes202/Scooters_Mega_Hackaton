1st prize for innovation at Sup MTI for Trotti Life Project Idea

29 May 2022




Our innovative project, titled "Trotti Life," was centered around the concept of electric scooter rentals. Given the growing global interest in sustainable transportation solutions, we envisioned an accessible and eco-friendly system that would allow individuals to rent electric scooters conveniently. We believed this idea had the potential to address urban mobility challenges while promoting environmental sustainability. The competition consisted of three selection rounds, and to our delight, we advanced through the first and second rounds successfully. However, during the final selection, our project faced challenges in securing funding due to the substantial financial requirements involved in its implementation. Despite this setback, we were immensely proud to have made it to the final selection alongside 40 other exceptional teams. Our school recognized our dedication and awarded our team the first prize for innovation, which was a testament to our teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. Through "Trotti Life," we aimed to contribute to the development of sustainable urban transportation solutions, aligning with the broader goals of reducing carbon emissions and promoting green mobility. While the project did not secure funding during the competition, we remain committed to pursuing innovative ideas that can make a positive impact on society.
